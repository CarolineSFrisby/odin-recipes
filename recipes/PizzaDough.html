Pizza Dough

Notes

  • longer prep time

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp dry active yeast
  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 1&1/3 cup of warm water
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbs olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix together warm water, sugar, and yeast and allow to sit till frothy
  2. Add in flour, salt, and olive oil
  3. Mix till a shaggy dough is formed
  4. Move to a flour covered surface and knead until smooth
  5. Place dough in clean oiled bowl and cover
  6. Place the bowl in a warm area and allow to double in size(approx. 90 min)
  7. Divide dough in two
  8. Cook now (or freeze for later and allow dough to come to room temp before cooking)
  9. Roll out your dough and place in oven at 350 for 5-7 minutes to pre-bake
  10. Add desired toppings and place back in oven till cheese is bubbly and crust is golden

Recommendation

My go to is a margherita pizza with extra cheese and pepperoni.

You'll need:

  • Pizza sauce (I like to sprinkle fresh minced garlic into my sauce)
  • Pizza blend cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Peperoni
  • Tomato
  • basil
