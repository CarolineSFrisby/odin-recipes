Caroline's Steak

Notes

  • 20 minutes total to prep and cook
  • <5 ingredients

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Garlic cloves
  • Desired seasonings
  • Rosemary sprigs (optional)

Steps

  1. Season steak with desired seasonings
  2. Heat drizzle of oil in pan on med/high
  3. Sear sides of steak for 2 minutes each
  4. Lower heat to med/low and add butter, garlic, and rosemary(optional) to pan
  5. Baste steak for additional 3-4 minutes
  6. Remove steak and allow to rest 5 minutes, then serve
